NASA has made an animation that shows how climate change might affect temperatures on different parts of Earth.

Temperatures around the world are going to increase, but some parts of the world will increase more than others.

The Northern Hemisphere will see the most dramatic temperature increases.

Especially over the Arctic and typically snowy parts of North America

Once ice and snow melts, it stops reflecting light away from Earth meaning the planet warms even more.

But the Southern Hemisphere will see warming too.

Land areas show the biggest warming, because evaporation can keep water cool.

And the oceans have the capacity to absorb more energy before heating up.
